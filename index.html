UI's Comparision

Manh Nguyen (s3999543)

website used:

  • Orb Farm
  • Limp Body Beat

Orb Farm is a Web-browser designed to simulate an Ecosystem inside a jar, the goal of this interaction is to grow and nurture a living environment in a virtual reality world. Meanwhile, Limb Body Beat, is an experience by Adult Swim, a beat generation web-site that generates beats by using a ragdoll.

1. Information Design

Orb Farm is a design is very minimalist in it own way, it styles is surrounding pixelated Retro Design look like from the 90s, one of the major notices is an empty circular pixel tank, and a clear control label on the top left of the sites with word like “Fish” or “Water”, and a O2/CO2 meter next to the buttons.

Limb Body Beat design is the opposite, its style is more 3D environment yet feels messy. It creates a dark, grimy and chaotic vibe about the worlds that you are inside of, however it still brings us the vibe that we want to explore and understand more about this world that they’re building.

The Design of Orb Farm is more relaxing, giving the chilling vibe in a family and friendly style, mostly more about directing and delivering clear messages of sorts. . Limb Body Beat is more of expression and exploration, it makes the users more curious of what is going on, the design itself taking an immersive experience, exposing itself more to the environment surrounding it.

2. Mapping

The interface is quite simple by using the actions of clicking and dragging, by doing that you spawn in the living organism to fill up the tank. You can easily track and navigate around, the Co2/o2 meters is visually in real-time showing you clearly of how you manage it. The one thing I like is the forward buttons next to the meter to speed up the process of the time.

Limb Body Beat is quite immersive, not only the interaction is by clicking on the beat-grid, but the users can drag the ragdoll around, by click and hold the surrounding space, the users can navigate around the environment, the ragdoll can also be drag to the cube the being projecting out to make some type of beat by collision.

The mapping in Orb Farm is more structured, and more simplified, aiming for guidance and easily understanding. Limb Body Beat is more about exploration, more exposure and taking curiosity to become the users own research.

3. Characterisation

Orb Farm takes its design with a calm visual feeling, especially the background and the whole visual is based mostly on a simple look, nothing too complicated, the design is aiming for educational purposes.

Adult Swims is really well known for a dark, edgy content. Limb Body Beat design is chaotic, sometimes making everything confused, that is why the whole experience felt weird and off, but because of the chaotic create interactive toy, where it still provokes users to continue to experience and keep exploring it.

Orb Farm is more about well structure and strongly more about simplifying making things more understandable and easy to learn. Limb Body Beat is more of a chaotic, complexity design, yet attracting the interaction into curiosity.

4. Feedback

For Orb Farm I think the CO2/O2 meter is a great feedback, because it represents a healthbar, like it gives a continuation of feedback that rapidly changes and keeps the core of what it needs to be. It is a case of learning and experimenting trying to balance the ecosystem's life in the simulation
For Limb Body Beat, I think the interaction between the ragdoll and the beat is the feedback of the whole interactive, by collision to each other, they both combine to generate sounds to make a beat. You don’t even get the messages or anything, only the ragdoll and the beat note is your feedback.
